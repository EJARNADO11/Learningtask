Pad Thai Chicken

4 servings

Ingredients

    Essential items:

  • Rice noodles (can use another type of noodle but read packet for intructions for how to cook)
  • 2 chicken breast
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 midium red chills (chopped)
  • 3 spring onions (chopped)
  • 2 eggs
  • Corriander (if I don't have any corriander i substitute for some dried mint)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 3 tbsp of fish sauce

Method

  1. Soak the rice noodles in hot water for 20 minutes or until cooked
  2. Heat some oil in a pan and add the two beaten and seasoned egg to make an omlette. Tranfer to a plate and chop into strips and leave to cool.
  3. Heat some oil in the work and add the chicken, once it is sealed on all sides add the garlic
  4. Add the spring onions and shrimp(if using) and stir fry for another minute
  5. Add the chillism drained noodles, fish sauce, lemon juice, sugar and peanuts and stir fry over a high heat for another minute
  6. Toss in the bean sprouts, omelette strips, mange tout and stir fry briskly for about 30 seconds
  7. Toss over corriander and season to taste. Serving piping hot.
